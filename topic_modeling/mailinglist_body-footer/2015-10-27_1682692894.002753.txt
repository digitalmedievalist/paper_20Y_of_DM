Manuscripts Project at Trinity College Dublin. Supported by the Bank of=20
America Merrill Lynch art conservation fund, the aim of the project is=20
to conserve, digitise and research four early Gospel Books made in Irish=20
monastic foundations: Codex Usserianus Primus (TCD MS 55),  the Book of=20
Mulling (TCD MS 60), the Book of Dimma (TCD MS 59), and the Garland of=20
Howth (TCD MS 56).To share with you progress and discoveries, we have just launched a blog=20
which will be our project diary. It will document the conservation,=20
digitisation and research processes through this year and well into the=20
next with text, photographs and videos posted each week.We hope you enjoy it! Please spread the word!http://www.tcd.ie/Library/early-irish-mss/Best wishes,CatherinePS: if you wish to be removed from this mailing list, just let me know.*The Early Irish Manuscripts blog is now=20
