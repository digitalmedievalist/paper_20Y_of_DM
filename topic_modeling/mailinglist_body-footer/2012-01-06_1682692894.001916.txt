exploring and exploiting TEI encoded data (Caen, 27/01/2012)For digital medievalists:
- papers given by *Alex Speed Kjeldsen* (Morpho-syntactic annotation of
ancient languages: The case of Old Norse), *Matthew James Driscoll
*(Quantitative
codicology) and *Lou Burnard *(Linguistic annotation).- tools presented by Serge *Heiden* (open-source plateform *TXM*),
Maud *Ingarao
*(*BaseX*, XML database)** and Marie *Bisson* (*Juxta *– comparison of
texts and *automated critical apparatus*).« Exploration et exploitation des sources encodées en TEI », Séminaire, *
Calenda*, publié le lundi 02 janvier 2012,
http://calenda.revues.org/nouvelle22190.htmlM. Dominique Stutzmann
