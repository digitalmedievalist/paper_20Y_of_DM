is offering a Marie Curie fellowship for an Experienced Researcher:=E2=80=9CTool integration in the digital edition=E2=80=9D
(full-time; fixed-term employment for 12 months, starting 1st November 2014).Specifications of the FellowshipThe research project should encompass one or more of the following topics:
- Study, assessment, evaluation, theorization of the integration and =20
possible enhancement of tools in digital editions in general
- Case studies (empirical survey, methodological discussion, sketch =20
for future developments, practical development and implementation) on =20
single fields of tool integration in scholarly editions or editorial =20
work environments, like personalization, rendering of text =20
presentation, image manipulation, transcription, annotation, text =20
analysis, geocoding, lexicon lookup, services for the integration of =20
external knowledge resources (e.g. controlled vocabularies, =20
bibliographic data)
- Visualization of textual relations in scholarly editions using =20
network analysis and visualization tools; connection of editions to =20
tools like Voyant, Juxta-Commons or CollateX etc.Professional Qualifications- Relevant doctoral degree (or at least four years of research experience)
- Preferably prior experience with digitization, tools in editing, =20
digital scholarship, scholarly editing.
- Fluency in English is required. Knowledge of German is not required.Formal RequirementsThis position is funded through the Marie Curie Initial Training =20
Network =E2=80=9CDiXiT=E2=80=9D. For details, see
http://dixit.uni-koeln.de.Applicants must
- be in possession of a doctoral degree or have at least four years of =20
full-time equivalent research experience.
- also have less than five years of full-time equivalent research experience.
- not have resided or carried out their main activity in the country =20
of their host organization (i.e. Germany) for more than 12 months in =20
the 3 years immediately prior to the reference date.
- be willing to spend up to 3 months of their tenure at the Huygens =20
Institute (Netherlands).Application Deadline: 15th August 2014Applications, including a letter of intent, CV, scan of diploma and =20
relevant certificates must be sent by e-mail to
dixit-info(a)uni-koeln.de.In addition, the mandatory DiXiT application form has to be filled in. =20
It can be found here:
http://dixit.uni-koeln.de/fellows.html
Follow the instructions!For further information contact the CCeH at
dixit-info(a)uni-koeln.de or +49 221 470 4056.
