> After having tried many software, including Bibdesk and Bookends, I've 
> recently been using Wikindx (http://wikindx.sourceforge.net, look under 
> "Features"), which is open-source and allows you to import from Bibtex 
> and Endnote as well as to export to RTF, RIS, Endnote, Bibtex and HTML. 
> All you need is a website with PHP and MySQL; it is thus possible to run 
> it on virtually every platform and to use it either locally or on the 
> Internet. And you can also use, edit and create any bibliographical 
> style you wish. For a complete list of features, see: 
> http://wikindx.sourceforge.net/features.html  There's even a multi-user 
> mode that can be useful for research groups.
