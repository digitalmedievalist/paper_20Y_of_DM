For Latin texts from the medieval Low Countries, I would suggest:
- Diplomata Belgica (www.diplomata-belgica.be): a corpus of +30.000 (mostly) =
Latin charters dated before 1250;- Narrative Sources (www.narrative-sources.be): a critical survey of narrativ=
e sources originating from the medieval Low Countries from the 6th to the fir=
st half of the 16th century
