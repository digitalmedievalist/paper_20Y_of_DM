=3D=3D=3D=3D=3DContest descriptionGlobal Outlook :: Digital Humanities, The University of Lethbridge,=20
Digital Studies/Le champ num=C3=A9rique, and The Alliance of Digital=20
Humanities Organisations is pleased to announce the first Global Digital=20
Humanities Essay Competition.=20
http://www.globaloutlookdh.org/global-outlookdigital-humanities-global-digita=
l-humanities-essay-prize/This is an open competition for research papers on the national,=20
regional, or international practice of the Digital Humanities--a broad=20
topic that has been designed to give authors the greatest possible=20
scope. Authors may write on individual projects or problems or broader=20
philosophical, geographical, sociological, political, or other aspects=20
of the practice of Digital Humanities in a global context. Papers=20
discussing the practice of DH by or with marginalised communities or in=20
areas that are currently less well represented by ADHO are particularly=20
welcome.The competition is open to any interested party including students,=20
graduate students, junior faculty, and researchers unaffiliated with a=20
university or research institution. We would like to especially=20
encourage submissions from students, junior and unaffiliated=20
researchers, and authors belonging to marginalised communities or=20
communities currently less well represented by ADHO.The competition is offering a minimum of 4 prizes of $500 (CAD) each.=20
Initial selection (for a prize of $200) is by abstract/proposal. A=20
further $300 will be awarded to the authors of the winning abstracts=20
upon satisfactory completion of a full-length paper based on their=20
original proposal. All submissions will be eligible for review and=20
publication in the ADHO journal, Digital Studies/Le champ num=C3=A9rique=20
(http://digitalstudies.org/).For further information about the competition, please see the=20
competition web page:=20
http://www.globaloutlookdh.org/global-outlookdigital-humanities-global-digita=
l-humanities-essay-prize/.=20
The competition organisers can also be contacted by email at=20
prizes(a)globaloutlookdh.orgThe initial deadline (abstracts/proposals) is June 30, 2013.-Daniel Paul O'Donnell
