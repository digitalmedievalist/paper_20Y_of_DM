Philology: A Journal of Medieval Cultures has released its inaugural issue.
This new venue for medieval studies published by the Johns Hopkins
University Press is accessible through Project Muse. You may reach Digital Philology's webpage, which includes the most current
Call for Submissions at:
http://www.press.jhu.edu/journals/digital_philology/index.html . You may access the first issue of the journal through your library's
subscription to Project Muse at:
http://muse.jhu.edu/journals/digital_philology/toc/dph.1.1.html . The HTML version of the Founding Editors' editorial statement is available
open access at:
http://muse.jhu.edu/journals/digital_philology/v001/1.1.nichols.html . All Best, Albert Lloret Albert Lloret, PhD
