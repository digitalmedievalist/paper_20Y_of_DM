(France) from Aug. 25th to Aug. 29th, 2015: *Reconstruct a medieval library
today: Saint-Bertin *(Reconstituer une bibliothéque m=C3=A9di=C3=A9vale =
aujourd'hui.
L'exemple de Saint-Bertin).The funding of Biblissima <http://biblissima-condorcet.fr/>, IRHT
<http://www.irht.cnrs.fr>, Saint-Omer
<http://www.bibliotheque-agglo-stomer.fr/> allows it to be free of charge
for participants (accomodation and meals included, no course fees).
Students will learn how to describe MSS (scientific description and TEI
encoding), how to scholarly edit inventories of MSS collections and how to
interconnect metadata from MSS descriptions and inventories to digitized
items, in order to better understand book production and use in the Middle
Ages and in the early modern period.Papers will be given in French. Students have to present a poster.Application until June 9th, 2015 ( application form)
<http://www.irht.cnrs.fr/sites/default/files/images/images_contenu/images_con=
tenu_site/pieces_jointes/formulaire_dinscription_saint-omer.pdf>.
Please circulate this information.Best regards,
Dominique StutzmannProgram*Mardi 25 ao=C3=BBt 2015 =E2=80=93 L'histoire des bibliothéques =
