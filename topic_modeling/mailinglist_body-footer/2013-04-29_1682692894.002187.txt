papers, application notes and demos for the 13th ACM Symposium on 
Document Engineering.* Short papers should report on smaller complete works of research or 
novel challenges or visions;
* application notes should describe systems or tools of interest to the 
DocEng community;
* posters should report on research in progress.Submissions should be of up to 4 pages in length (2 pages for posters). 
  The following deadlines apply:* May 19: Abstracts due
* May 22: Manuscripts dueThe ACM Symposium on Document Engineering provides an annual 
international forum for presentations and discussions on principles, 
tools and processes that improve our ability to create, manage and 
maintain documents.  You can find the Call for Papers here:https://sites.google.com/site/doceng2013/call-for-papersAll papers should be submitted on the EasyChair site.  You can find the 
submission instructions here:https://sites.google.com/site/doceng2013/call-for-papers/submission-proceduresLooking forward to seeing you at DocEng 2013.Best regards,Tamir Hassan
University of Konstanz, Germany
