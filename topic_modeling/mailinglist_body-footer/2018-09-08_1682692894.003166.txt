a special number devoted to the Storia de Espanna Digital, a digital archive
dedicated to the late thirteenth-century chronicle of Spain directed by King
Alfonso X of Castile. Guest-edited by Aengus Ward, DPh 7.1 includes articles
by Barbara Bordalejo, Heather Bamford and Emily Francomano, Polly Duxfield,
and Aengus Ward. It also contains an essay by Brian J. Reilly on authorship
verification through modern translations of Old French texts. Here is the
TOC: http://muse.jhu.edu/issue/38874 .  Enjoy! Albert  Albert Lloret, Ph.D.Associate Professor of Spanish and Catalan
