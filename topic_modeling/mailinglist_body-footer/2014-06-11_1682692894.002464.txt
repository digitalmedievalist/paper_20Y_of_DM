Neo-Latin poetry in English manuscripts, 1550-1700ALL WELCOMEThis paper discusses a proposed project to examine the role and=20
significance of the large quantities of neo-Latin poetry composed and=20
circulated within the thriving manuscript culture of early modern=20
England (c. 1550-1700). It will produce a searchable digital edition of=20
representative examples of early modern Latin poetry in English=20
manuscripts, and a body of print publications analysing this almost=20
unstudied wealth of material. We address the typical genres and forms of=20
neo-Latin poetry in manuscript and how they are used; the relationship=20
between original Latin and English poetry in manuscript sources; and the=20
political significance of such poetry.The seminar will be followed by wine and refreshments.For more information please contact Gabriel.Bodard(a)kcl.ac.uk,=20
