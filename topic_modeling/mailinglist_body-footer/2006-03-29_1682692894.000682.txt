        our members (apologies for cross posting):XML and TEI: IntroductionThursday, May 4, 10:00 a.m.-11:30 a.m.Session 59: Waldo Library Classroom A
Instructor: James Cummings, Oxford Text ArchiveThis workshop offers an introduction to best practices for digital
scholarship, taught by a medievalist, James Cummings, specifically for
medievalists.  Instruction includes: introductory-level XML and
structural encoding, as well as new TEI P5 standards and guidelines,
markup concerns for medieval transcription, manuscript description
metadata, XML Editors, including assignments to be completed during
clinic (11:30 - 12:30).Limited to 33 participants, registration is required (for info:
pcossard(a)umd.edu).  Fee is $50 for pre-registrants, $60 for walk-ins.
Please mail c/o Patti Cossard, Architecture Library, University of
Maryland, College Park, MD 20742.  Include your contact information and
check for fee payable to Medieval Academy of America.
        
XML and TEI: Clinic IThursday, May 4, 11:30 a.m.-12:30 p.m.
Waldo Library Classroom A
        
This clinic immediately follows the "XML and TEI: Introduction" workshop
(10:00 - 11:30 a.m.), assignments from that class can be completed
during this proctored clinic.
        
XML and TEI: Advanced TEI P5 Customization and Transformation
Thursday, May 4, 3:30 p.m.-5:00 p.m.Waldo Library Classroom A
Instructor: James Cummings, Oxford Text ArchiveThis workshop offers advanced instruction in customization and
transformation of TEI encoded XML files, taught by a medievalist, James
Cummings, specifically for medievalists.  Instruction includes:
advanced-level concepts of TEI P5 modularization and customization for
individual projects, schemas, XSLT for XML transformation, Xquery and
XML Databases, including assignments to be completed during clinic (5:00
- 6:00).Limited to 33 participants, registration is required (for info:
pcossard(a)umd.edu).  Fee is $50 for pre-registrants, $60 for walk-ins.
Please mail c/o Patti Cossard, Architecture Library, University of
Maryland, College Park, MD 20742.  Include your contact information and
check for fee payable to Medieval Academy of America.
