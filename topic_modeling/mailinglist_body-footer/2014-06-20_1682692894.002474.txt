to announce the launch of a new review journal, RIDE. We aim to make =20
digital editions and resources more visible and to provide a forum in =20
which expert peers can evaluate and discuss the efforts of digital =20
editors in order to improve current practices and advance future =20
developments. It will do so by asking its reviewers to pay attention =20
not only to the traditional virtues and vices of any edition, but also =20
to the evolving methodology and its technical implications.You can access all reviews at: http://ride.i-d-e.deYou can find out more about the ideas behind RIDE, our methods and =20
intentions, in the Editorial at: http://ride.i-d-e.de/about/editorial/We are also looking for contributions. Please see: =20
http://ride.i-d-e.de/reviewers/Enjoy the ride!
