Marco Institute for Medieval and Renaissance Studies at the  
University of Tennessee, Knoxville on Friday and Saturday, February  
3-4, 2006. Scholars and students at any rank who are interested in  
learning more about textual criticism and the work of editing are  
invited to attend.     In individual ninety-minute sessions, eight scholars will  
present their works in progress; they will discuss the text and its  
context, explain the form of their edition, and share their ideas on  
any aspect of the theory and practice of editing.        Information on the schedule and a registration form are now
