Initial Training Network (DiXiT) has been awarded a multi-million Euro =20
European grant for investigating the creation and publication of =20
digital scholarly editions.DiXiT is an international network of high-profile institutions from =20
the public and the private sector offering a coordinated training and =20
research programme for early stage researchers and experienced =20
researchers in the multi-disciplinary skills, technologies, theories, =20
and methods of digital scholarly editing.The programme includes twelve fellowships for early stage researchers =20
(PhD students) for a period of three years, and five fellowships for =20
experienced researchers (Post-Docs) for a period of 12 to 20 months. =20
The positions will be widely advertised starting from the month of =20
October.DiXiT will also organise six training events at various levels (camps =20
& conventions), open to all DiXiT fellows as well as to other people =20
that may be interested in the topics covered.The DiXiT network includes the following academic partners:
